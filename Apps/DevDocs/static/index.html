Make your own viewer!

  1. Connect your GitHub account.
  2. Fork the Skeleton Viewer.
  3. Edit the index.html file on GitHub. After line 11 of index.html add:
    body { background-color:black; }
  4. Manually sync your viewer back into your Singly account.

Great! Your viewer will now appear in the Photos drop down menu!
